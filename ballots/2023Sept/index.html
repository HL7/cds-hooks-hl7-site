    September 2023 Hook Maturity Ballot

    Info

    For this ballot, only the hooks are under revision. Feedback is specifically solicited on the proposed maturities of the CDS Hooks hooks, per the CDS Hooks Maturity Model.

    The September 2023 ballot period is open from August 4th, 2023 through September 4th, 2023. The September 2023 ballot includes the library of CDS Hooks, most of which have never before been balloted. Specifically:

    • patient-view ballot at Normative, previously at maturity level 5 - Mature.
    • order-sign initial ballot at Trial Use, maturity level 5
    • order-select intial ballot at Trial Use, maturity level 4
    • appointment-book intitial ballot at Trial Use, maturity level 3
    • encounter-discharge intial ballot at Trial Use, maturity level 3
    • encounter-start initial ballot at Trial Use, maturity level 1

    Ballot comments can be submitted per normal HL7 processes. Submitters are encouraged to submit using the HL7 Jira issue tracker, setting the Specification of submitted issues to CDS Hooks.
